Principal Investigator

Lab Director

RL

Dr. Ryun Jung Lee

Assistant Professor · School of Architecture and Planning, UTSA

Dr. Lee's research focuses on the intersection of urban design, GIS, and environmental justice. He uses geospatial methods to examine how the built and natural environment affects quality of life in socially vulnerable communities across San Antonio and beyond.

His work spans built environment analysis, green infrastructure, climate adaptation, and social vulnerability — with active partnerships with the City of San Antonio and community organizations.
