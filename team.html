RL

Ryun Jung (RJ) Lee, Ph.D., AICP

Assistant Professor of Urban and Regional Planning · UTSA

Member of the American Institute of Certified Planners and core faculty of the Center for Urban and Regional Planning Research at UTSA. His research examines the links between built environment, neighborhood change, and quality of life through geospatial analysis, with a focus on community resilience and environmental justice.
